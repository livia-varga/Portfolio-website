UI design plans made in Figma

Cut & Color Co. Hair Salon

This is a hair salon app that I designed in Figma. I tried to give clear signals to the users so that they can book as soon as possible, with short messages and arrow keys illustrating what the next step is. In terms of colors, black, white and red dominate. I wanted to create a young, modern, expressive look.

Nigiri Sushi Application

This is a sushi ordering application that I designed it in Figma. Green and white colors dominate, which I wanted to symbolize cleanliness and freshness and that sushi is a healthy food. In my plan, a dropdown menu appears on the main page, from where the user can easily find information in the application. Next comes the Menu, where you can choose from different sushi and a search function is also displayed. On the product selection page, you can add additional accessories to your order. Finally, the user pays and orders the product on the cart page. I think it's an easy-to-interpret, transparent page.

Rhythm Wave

This is a web player desktop landing page. I wanted to achieve a clean look. A page where users can easily navigate to find their favorite music. This is the starting page, further development is expected in terms of sub-pages.
